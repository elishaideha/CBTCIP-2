Frequently Asked Questions

We provide valuable insights into our products, services, and the industrial landscape.This comprehensive resource is designed to answer your most common queries

How long does it take to sell a property?

The time it takes to sell a property depends on various factors, such as the property's condition, location, price, and market conditions. On average, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months.

How do I choose the right estate agent for my needs?

Look for an agent with relevant experience, good reputation, and local knowledge. Check their track record, ask for recommendations, and compare their fees and services.

What happens if the property sale falls through?

If a sale falls through, you may need to start the process again with a new buyer. However, if the issue lies with the buyer, you may be entitled to compensation for any costs incurred. Consult with your estate agent and solicitor for guidance.
